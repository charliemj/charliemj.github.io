Undergraduate Research in MIT's Creative Communities Initiative

I was an undergraduate researcher in the Creative Communities Initiative from fall 2014 to fall 2016. I've used ethnographic and data-driven methods to study television fans on Tumblr. I wrote Python scripts to collect post data from the Tumblr site. Using the scraper, users can select a given tag and specify a time range to gather all posts with that tag in that time range. Eventually, I'd like to make some interactive data visualizations to show off this data, particularly to see around what times during a TV show season fans are most active with certain fan activities. I spent a lot of time in this position conducting ethnographic studies on fans who use Tumblr.
