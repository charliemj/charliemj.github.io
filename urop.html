Undergraduate Research in MIT's Creative Communities Initiative

I've been an undergraduate researcher in the Creative Communities Initiative since fall 2015. I've been using ethnographic and data-driven methods to study television fans on Tumblr. I've written Python scripts to collect post data from the Tumblr site. Users can select a given tag and specify a time range to gather all posts with that tag in that time range. Eventually, I'd like to make some interative data visualizations to show off this data, particularly to see around what times during a TV show season fans are most active with certain fan activities. I spent a lot of time in this position conducting ethnographic studies on fans who use Tumblr.
